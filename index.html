About US

Haven AI is a groundbreaking platform that combines blockchain and artificial intelligence (AI) to deliver Customer Support as a Service (CSaaS). Businesses can leverage Haven AI to offer their customers support through phone calls, SMS, and a customizable web SDK. By merging decentralized technology with AI-driven automation, Haven AI redefines how businesses interact with their customers while ensuring security, scalability, and accessibility. At the core of the ecosystem is the HAI token, which powers the platform and its services.
